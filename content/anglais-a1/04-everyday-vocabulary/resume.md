# 📜 Résumé: Everyday Vocabulary

- **Family** — _mother / father → parents_, _sister / brother_, _son / daughter_, _aunt / uncle_, _niece / nephew_, _grandmother / grandfather_; a _cousin_ is the child of an aunt or uncle.
- **Numbers** — cardinals count (_one, two, three…_); ordinals order (_first, second, third_).
- **Days & months** — seven days (_Monday … Sunday_), twelve months (_January … December_), always **capitalized**.
- **Colours** — _red, blue, green, yellow, black, white, orange, purple, brown, grey, pink_.
- **Home & school objects** — _table, chair, door, window, key, phone_; _book, pen, pencil, bag, desk_.
- **Jobs** — _teacher, doctor, nurse, driver, cook/chef, farmer, engineer_.
- **Food & drink** — _bread, rice, apple, egg_; _water, milk, coffee, tea_; _a glass of water_, _a cup of tea_.
- **Basic adjectives & opposites** — _big ↔ small, hot ↔ cold, happy ↔ sad, old ↔ new, cheap ↔ expensive, fast ↔ slow, easy ↔ difficult_.
- ⚠️ Don't mix the pairs: _aunt_ (female) ≠ _uncle_ (male); _niece_ (girl) ≠ _nephew_ (boy); a _pen_ (ink) ≠ a _pencil_ (lead).
